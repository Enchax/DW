About Us

Lucid Air is a developmental community based non-profit organisation in New York founded in 2019 with purpose of addressing rural development issues and natural resource management and to empower people to take action to address the air quality issues we face.

Volunteer

Volunteers are utilized in many areas, some of which include distribution of educational information and Campaign for Lucid Air events.
